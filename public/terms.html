Welcome to the DAK Register Web Application, developed and hosted by the National Informatics Centre (NIC), Meghalaya State Center, Shillong. By using this application, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. Registration and Account Creation

Users must register by providing accurate first name, last name, and phone number. A CAPTCHA verification is required to prevent automated registrations. Duplicate registrations, including variations in capitalization, are not permitted. The phone number must contain only numerical digits. Your account is personal and should not be shared.

2. Login and Data Access

Login requires the registered phone number. Upon successful login, users can access their Dispatch and Acquired logbook tables, with data loaded from a PostgreSQL database specific to their account.

3. Logbook Features

The application provides tools for managing Dispatch and Acquired logs, including:

  • Font customization (size, style, bold, underline, italics).
  • Find and replace functionality.
  • View up to 100 rows (default 6 rows).
  • Inbuilt PDF viewer.
  • Column-specific search and filter (ascending/descending).
  • Add rows via button or right-click (above/below existing rows).
  • Delete rows via right-click.
  • Switch between Dispatch and Acquired tables.

All data is stored securely in a PostgreSQL database.

4. Usage Restrictions

The application is intended solely for logging Dispatch and Acquired records. Misuse, such as entering invalid data or attempting to bypass security measures (e.g., CAPTCHA), may result in account suspension. Users must comply with NIC Meghalaya's guidelines and applicable laws.

5. Data Privacy

Your personal information (first name, last name, phone number) is stored securely in a PostgreSQL database and will not be shared without your consent, except as required by law or for operational purposes by NIC Meghalaya.

6. Modifications to Terms

NIC Meghalaya reserves the right to update these terms. Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of any changes. Check this page periodically for updates.

7. Contact Information

For questions or support, contact the National Informatics Centre, Meghalaya State Center, Shillong.
